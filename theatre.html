Nangman Theatre is an independent musical theatre company founded in Seoul in 2016. Selected as Seoul Youth Arts Group (서울청년예술단) in 2017, with ₩50,000,000 in public funding. The company creates and produces original work rooted in Korean storytelling, with an international perspective shaped by collaborations across South Korea, the United Kingdom, and the Middle East.

As Founder and Artistic Director, Hyojung Jung leads the creative vision of each production — from dramaturgy and direction through to performance, often inhabiting multiple roles in a single work.

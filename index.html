Seoul  ·  London  ·  Riyadh

Korean actor, theatre director, and cultural arts educator working between Saudi Arabia, the UK, and South Korea. Founder and Artistic Director of Nangman Theatre, Seoul (est. 2016).

Cultural Affairs Instructor and Events Coordinator at King Sejong Institute, Prince Sultan University, Riyadh — where she also conducts a Saudi women's choir. Saudi Arabia correspondent for KOFICE and guest correspondent for KOFIC. Guest speaker, Korean Film Week Saudi Arabia 2025.

Currently MA student at the Shakespeare Institute, University of Birmingham, researching Korean productions of Shakespeare.
